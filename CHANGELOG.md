# Changelog

See more at https://github.com/brgmnn/gtfo-killable-spitters

## v0.1.0 — July 14, 2026

- Initial release. Infection spitters can be killed with bullets (default 30 hp
  health pool) and C-foam (default 5 seconds after being foamed). Deaths are
  host-authoritative and synced to all players, including late joiners.
- Includes a fix so spitters no longer aggro onto AI bot teammates, with
  support for >4-player lobbies.
